Sunset Smoothie

Editorial Team at Kitchen Stories

Difficulty Preparation Baking Resting
Easy 👌 20 min 0 min 0 min

Ingredients

2 Servings

  • 6 strawberries
  • 5 mint leaves
  • 600 g watermelon
  • 1 lime
  • 4 ice cubes

Nutrition per servising

Cal Fat Protein Carb
143 1g 3g 31 g

Step 1/2

  • 6 strawberries (large) 5 mint leaves 600 g watermelon
  • cutting-boardknife
Quarter strawberries and pick mint leaves. Cut chilled watermelon into pieces.

Step 2/2

  • 1 lime-4 ice cubes
  • citrus press-blender
Add strawberry, watermelon, lime juice, ice cubes, and mint leaves to blender. Blend until smooth for approx. 2 - 3 min. Enjoy while cold.
